# What's in the box

1. In the box
    - Bespoke Biltong Dryness Scale (BB1).
    - Power cable to plug into the distribution board on the BB1 and BB3 driers.
2. Unboxing and connecting the power supply
    1. Place the BB1 unit on your dryer of choice.
    2. Use the supplied power cable to connect to the Micro USB connector on the right hand side of the BB1 unit.
    3. Connect the standard USB connector side to a 5V USB type charger that can deliver at least 0.3A.
